Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of software developers eager to share ideas and innovations. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most influential technology conferences in the world, bringing together tech leaders, innovators, and enthusiasts from across the globe.

Each year, TechCon continues to push the boundaries of technology discussions, focusing on emerging trends such as AI, cybersecurity, green tech, and more.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and empower individuals and organizations to leverage technology for positive change.

We believe in the transformative power of technology to solve real-world problems and shape a sustainable and inclusive future.
